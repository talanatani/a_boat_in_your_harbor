Invocation

to boundless love: to choose tenderness over performance to remain open when it would be easier to close to hold what is holy without possession ethereality to move through the world like breath through linen to let the unseen be felt without being named bearing witness to look without consuming to honor what is offered transformation to let love change the shape of the one who touches it to ritual: to return again and again until the heart remembers

Your pain is the breaking of the shell that encloses your understanding

Even as the stone of the fruit must break that its heart may stand in the sun so must you know pain

— Kahlil Gibran
