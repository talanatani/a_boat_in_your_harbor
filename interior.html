i felt my skin bathed in golden light,

an external sense which was
so much more deeply felt
through the gratitude
of my internal world.

i felt love surrounding me,
so intensely and densely
it could have been packed
in all four corners of the room.

my body and spirit surrendered
in a way i had always before
reserved for god, my creator —
it felt free.

and it did not go away,
but stayed
as a new standard of love
that the world has to offer.

a new low exists as well.
and yet this memory
brings me back.

To ritual.
To boundless love.

I call you into my life
as a gift
to all others.
